Whitepaper

Fluffys are NFT characters on the blockchain who plant the forest and take care of it. Fluff's NFT will allow users to both earn and plant a REAL FOREST!

Each owner of the NFT collection will receive a FREE NFT plot of land on which it will be possible to plant an NFT forest!

1 NFT tree planted on this plot of land = one real tree planted!

The owner of Fluff's NFT will be able to passively receive income, because he will also be the owner of the NFT plot and 10% of the commission for the mint of NFT trees will be distributed among the owners of the plots.

The mint of NFT trees will be available directly on the website, the cost of the mint will consist of the cost of planting a tree (funds will be sent to organizations engaged in planting trees), a commission of 10% to the owner of the site and a commission of 10% to the site.

Anyone can mine NFT trees, but he will have to choose any of the 10,000 plots on which he will plant it.

Owners of Flaffys NFT can sell both Flaffys NFT itself and separately an NFT plot on the secondary market with trees planted on it.

The more trees are planted on the NFT site, the more expensive it can be sold in theory.
